# sales-performance-dashboard
## Overview
Interactive Excel Dashboard analysing 9,994 rows of US retail sales data (2014/2017)
Built to identify profit leakage, regional underperformance and discount impact
## Tools
Microsoft Excel 2024 | Power Query | Pivot Tables | Slicers
## Key Findings
- Furniture profit margins are 2.5 vs 17.4% for Technology
- Central Region margin{7.9%} is nearly half the West {14.9%}
- Orders with >40% discount are consistently making losses
- Tables and Books cases subcategories generate negative profit

## Business Recommendations
1. Cap furniture at 20% to recover an estimated $40K+ in annual profit
2. Audit Central region pricing and cost structure
3. Increase investment in Technology sales -- highest margin category
